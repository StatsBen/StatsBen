benjamindavidclark.com Privacy Policy

No users other than myself will be granted an account to this website. Don't try to log in, nothing will happen (except that I'll be able to see that you've tried). The only reason I've even implemented a log in at all is so that I can manage my own website remotely. There shall be no other users besides myself, and I will store no data on any other users, except in the event in which they attempt to log in, in which case all that will be stored is whatever random crap Google decides to collect (because they're running the sign-in service).
